Who Are We

The Puerto Rico Water Resources and Environmental Research Institute (PRWRERI) stands as one of 54 prestigious institutes in the United States committed to researching and providing solutions to challenges in water resources and environmental preservation. With over 50 years of experience in the field, PRWRERI serves as a trusted consultant to a wide array of government agencies, municipalities, federal entities, and both public and private corporations. Our extensive expertise is bolstered by years of cutting-edge research and education, coupled with a vast repository of resources and knowledge. Through our dedication and hard work, we set the standard for future generations in the sustainable management of water and the environment.
