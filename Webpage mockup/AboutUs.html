About Us

Our Mission

The Puerto Rico Water Resources and Environmental Research Institute (PRWRERI) is one of 54 water research centers established throughout the United States and its territories by The Water Research Resource Act of 1964 and presently operates under Section 104 of the Water Research and Development Act of 1984 (P.L. 98-242). Sponsored by federal and university funds, PRWRERI undertakes a variety of research projects aimed at addressing water resources and environmental issues in Puerto Rico.

Translating Research Into Practical Applications

The Institute serves as the hub for water-related research in Puerto Rico, through meetings, seminars, reports, and newsletters, it keeps the community informed about research advancements. Additionally, it organizes major conferences every two years, fostering collaboration with regional organizations.
